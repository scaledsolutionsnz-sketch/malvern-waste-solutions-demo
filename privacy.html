Privacy Policy

What we collect, and how we look after it.

Malvern Waste Solutions respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle the information you share with us. This is a demonstration website; the final policy will be confirmed before launch.

What we collect

When you enquire or book, we collect the details you give us — typically your name, phone number, email and the address or suburb where the work is needed. We only ask for what we need to quote and carry out the job.

How we use it

We use your information to respond to enquiries, provide quotes, schedule collections, and stay in touch about your service. We don't sell your information to anyone.

Sharing

We only share your details where it's needed to deliver the service (for example, with a team member completing your collection) or where required by New Zealand law.

Form submissions

Enquiries sent through this website are delivered to us by email via a third-party form service. They're used solely to respond to your request.

Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you can ask to see the information we hold about you and request corrections. Get in touch and we'll help.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Call (03) 318 7407 or email info@mws.co.nz.
