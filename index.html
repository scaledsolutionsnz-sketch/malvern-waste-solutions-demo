Who we are

A local waste service that actually turns up.

Malvern Waste Solutions has been keeping the Selwyn District tidy for over two decades. What started as Malvern Bins is now the region's go-to rubbish and recycling contractor — trusted by households, lifestyle blocks, farms and main-street businesses from Darfield to Rolleston.

We're owned and run by Kristen and Mick O'Callaghan, so when you call you're talking to someone who knows your road, your run and your bin. No franchise runaround — just a dependable local service that shows up on the day it says it will.

  • Reliable weekly & fortnightly scheduled runs
  • Domestic, rural, commercial & event waste
  • Recycling kept out of landfill wherever we can
