Terms of Service

The basics of how we work together.

These terms apply to waste collection, skip bin hire and clean-up services provided by Malvern Waste Solutions across the Malvern district and wider Selwyn, Canterbury. This is a demonstration website; final terms will be confirmed in writing with each customer.

Quotes & bookings

Quotes are based on the information you provide about the job. If the work or volume of waste differs from what was described, we'll talk to you before any change in price. Bookings are confirmed once we've agreed a collection or drop-off date with you.

Skip bin hire

Hired bins remain the property of Malvern Waste Solutions. Please keep loads level and within the bin, and don't place prohibited items (hazardous materials, liquids, gas bottles, asbestos or chemicals) in any bin. Overloaded or contaminated bins may incur additional charges.

Collections

We aim to collect on the agreed day and will let you know if a collection needs to be rescheduled due to weather, access or other circumstances. Please ensure bins are accessible and kerbside by the morning of collection.

Payment

Payment terms are agreed at the time of booking. Charges reflect the service, bin size and waste type quoted.

Liability

We take care on every site. To the extent permitted by the Consumer Guarantees Act 1993 and other applicable New Zealand law, our liability is limited to the cost of the service provided.
